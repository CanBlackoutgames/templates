## Groups and Slots

The composition of the groups is based on the choice of the vehicle for the given group.
Whatever happens, there must be **exactly 33 players** per side.

| ![USA](http://www.ofcrav2.org/images/publiques/flag_usa.jpg) | ![Russia](http://www.ofcrav2.org/images/publiques/flag_russia.jpg) |
|:-------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| ![usa](http://www.ofcrav2.org/images/publiques/campagne/mangusta/usa_groups.png) | ![russia](http://www.ofcrav2.org/images/publiques/campagne/mangusta/ru_groups.png) |
| **Example:**<br>Commander is mandatory, let's consider the 32 remaining slots and the 3 RS first:<br><br>I choose 1x M-117 for RS1, 1x RG-33 for RS2, 1x (2x M-1025) for RS3 and for the remaining slots, we will see later our options.<br>**RS 1:** M-117 has 2x ground crews + 5x infantry units => I must select the 5 first slots into the RS.<br>**RS 2:** RG-33 has 8 infantry units without crew<br>**RS 3:** M-1025 (M2) + M-1025 (MK19) have  8x infantry units without crew<br>**RS 4:** I already have 23 slots within the 3 first RS., it lacks 32 - 23 = 9 slots.<br><br>I cannot choose the M-113, because it requires 10 slots. I can choose any vehicle that takes 9 slots or less. Of course, I must have enough vehicles in regards to the restrictions. I choose here 1x M2A3.<br><br>After my 4 RS completed, It still lacks 1 slot. This is why the **Completion group** exists: you have to select the right number of slots to get 32 slots (+1 Platoon Sergeant) selected.<br><br>So, I will have an additional Squad Leader in *Whiskey* that can help the Platoon Sergeant or join another RS.<br>Completion Group has 1x M-1097 without any other possible choice.<br><br>In the end, my Orders of Battles are:<br>![usa](http://www.ofcrav2.org/images/publiques/campagne/mangusta/usa_groups_example_01.png)<br><br>**Remarks:**<br>- The Platoon Sergeant has 1x M-1097-half (6 seats) for himself<br>- If RS1 and RS2 were equipped with M-113, the RS3 would be disabled (you can use a RS **only** if you can select a vehicle and the exact number of slots that the vehicle requires, as described above), and the 4 missing slots would be the 4 first ones in the Completion Group.<br>- The 4 RS can be used with of 7, 8 or 10 slots only.<br>- In case of UH-60M, choose aircrew team instead of ground crew. | **Example:**<br>Commander is mandatory, let's consider the 24 remaining slots and the 4 RS first:<br>I choose 1x Mi-8MT for RS 1, 1x BRDM-2 for RS2 2, and for the remaining slots, we will see later our options.<br><br>**RS 1:** Mi-8MT has 2x aerial crews + 7 infantry units => I must select all of them.<br>**RS 2:** BRDM-2 has 2x ground crews + 7 infantry units => I must select all of them.<br>**RS 3:** I still have to select 6 slots, so I cannot use this group, because it can be used with 7 or 9 slots only.<br><br>After my 3 RS completed, It still lacks 1 slot. This is why the **Completion group** exists: you have to select the right number of slot to get 24 slots (+1 Platoon Sergeant) selected.<br>So, I will have *VICTOR* with 6 units.<br>Completion group has 1x UAZ-open, no other possible choice.<br><br>In the end, my Orders of Battles are:<br>![img](http://www.ofcrav2.org/images/publiques/campagnes/mangusta/ru_groups_example_01.png)<br><br>**Remarks:**<br>- The Platoon Sergeant has 1x UAZ (7 seats) for himself<br>- The 4 RS can be used with 7 or 9 slots only. |
